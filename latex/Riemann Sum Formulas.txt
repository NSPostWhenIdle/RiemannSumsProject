Right

\Delta x [f(a + \Delta x)  + f(a + 2\Delta x) + \cdots  + f(b)]

Middle

\Delta x [f(a + \tfrac{\Delta x}{2})  + f(a + \tfrac{3\Delta x}{2}) + \cdots  + f(b - \tfrac{\Delta x}{2})]

Trapezoidal

\frac{\Delta x}{2}  [f(a) + 2f(a + \Delta x)  + 2f(a + 2\Delta x) + 2f(a + 3\Delta x) + \cdots  + f(b)]
